Isaac Newton

(1642 - 1727)

Sir Isaac Newton was an English mathematician, physicist, astronomer, alchemist, theologian, and author who was described in his time as a natural philosopher. He was a key figure in the Scientific Revolution.

Important dates

  • 1665 Newton discovers the generalized binomial theorem.
  • 1666 Newton experiments with sunlight and prisms. He discovered that clear white light was composed of seven visible colors.
  • 1687 Newton publishes Principia, which outlines his three laws of motion and his law of gravitation.
  • 1696 Newton becomes the warder of the Mint and takes charge of England's great recoining.
  • 1705 Newton was knighted by Queen Anne.
